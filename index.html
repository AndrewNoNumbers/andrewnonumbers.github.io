About

Andrew Chen is a T-shaped Senior Product Designer, comfortable driving vision and inspiring teams as much as managing design systems, aligning stakeholders, and leading customer research. He has shipped features used by millions at companies like Zest Dermatology, New Relic, Curology, and Alation. Outside of design, he enjoys working on his car and biking around San Francisco.
